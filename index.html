    Khumbu

    Nepal

    Khumbu is a magnet for mountaineers and trekking enthusiasts. The treacherous Khumbu Icefall, a labyrinth of crevasses and towering glaciers, leads to Everest Base Camp, a bustling hub for expeditions. Many other trekking routes offer stunning panoramas of the Himalayas, passing through charming Sherpa villages and ancient Buddhist monasteries.

While most famous for vast sand dunes, the Sahara also boasts rocky plateaus, salt flats, and even mountains. Despite the harshness, life persists.
